Stanford Technology Summer camp

SIGN UP

Overview

The Stanford Summer Camp is designed for 12-17 year olds who love innovation while willing to explore the unknown and dare to challenge themselves. The summer camp will be based on the learning from the outstanding people and the vision of the future, and will focus on artificial intelligence, VR / AR, robotics, 3D printing and other aspects.


In two weeks, students will not only receive in-person guidance from the world's top professors, but also experience the charm of North America's top summer camp with students from all over the world. They will also be having seminars with the best young entrepreneurs, touring to the most famous innovative technology enterprises, fully experiencing entrepreneurship culture.

Highlights

  • Top interactive courses: clear topics, covering the most popular technology innovation courses, students will create their own course works
  • Immersive learning experience: live in university dormitories with students from all over the world and experience the daily life of American college
  • Class teaching by top instructors : Stanford Professors offer high-quality courses in order to lets them experience the charm of academics
  • Discussion with Outstanding Young Entrepreneurs: Face to face talk to Forbes 30 under30 award-winning entrepreneurs and feel the power of role models
  • Zero-distance contact with wonderful entrepreneurial culture: Deepen into the most representative American innovative company and entrepreneurship education base, and experience the entrepreneurial culture
  • Family moment: We invite parents to participate in the camping ceremony and share the moment of student growth
