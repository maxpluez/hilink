Outstanding Faculties

Instructor: Dr. Zhenyi Xu, former director of Office of Asian Affairs in the National University of Singapore. He graduated with a PhD from the National University of Singapore and Nanjing University.


He is currently the managing director of Singapore Eye, the largest Chinese language media in Singapore, and the vice president of the Nanyang Society. In the past, he used to spend nine years in China, serving as the Singapore Consul in Shanghai and Nanjing.
