German Equestrian Course

Ismail Racecourse is Europe's largest thoroughbred Arabian racecourse. It is located in the picturesque 200-hectare private estate of the Schneider Zoo in Germany. The owner of the racetrack comes from a famous equestrian family with more than 300 years of Equestrian experience. The racecourse transports excellent breeds of Arabian horses to significant events every year and has won numerous awards. The horses it has cultivated have been purchased by the Arab chiefs and given to Chinese leaders Xi Jinping as gifts.


The racecourse also provides customized equestrian courses for students of all ages and levels. The standard project is 6 days, including 5 days of equestrian classes and one day of cultural activities. The specific content can be customized according to customer needs.

Highlights

  • Ismail Racecourse is Europe's largest private Arabian horse farm, covering an area of more than 200 hectares, currently breeds nearly 60 purebred Arabian mares and approximately 15 stallions
  • Germany is one of the world's leading equestrian powers. The German Equestrian Association (FN) seats the highest organization of German equestrian sports and is also the world's largest equestrian association
