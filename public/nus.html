National University of Singapore Summer Camp

SIGN UP

Overview

This program provides world-class prestigious financial innovation courses for young people aged 14 to 20, where students will learn business knowledge in depth, experience youth leadership training at the seaside, get to know well-known Singaporean companies, and visit the designated wildlife protection organization ACRES ...

Highlights

  • Take business and leadership courses at National University of Singapore
  • Overseas wildlife protection volunteer opportunities
  • Youth leadership training
  • Learn at well-known Singaporean companies (United Overseas Bank, Mediacorp, and NEWwater)

Courses

Banking and Finance

  • Learn about Singapore's financial market development strategy for China and neighboring countries, sustainable financial development strategies, and structural challenges and opportunities of financial markets in Asia Pacific

Smart City

  • Learn about what a smart city is, understand the changes that smart cities will make to business and society in the future, and the disruptive areas of innovation that smart cities have

Leadership and Management

  • In-depth understanding of what leadership is, how to build a cohesive team and cultivate leadership in cross-cultural communication
